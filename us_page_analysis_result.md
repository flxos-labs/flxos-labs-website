## 💡 New Feature Ideas

### 🎮 Interactive / Game Sections

#### 1. **Love Quiz / "How Well Do You Know Me?"**
- A set of multiple-choice questions about each other (favorite color, favorite memory, etc.)
- Tracks score, shows a cute result screen ("Soulmate Level: 100% 🥰")
- **Difficulty:** Medium

#### 2. **Scratch Card / Mystery Reveal**
- A canvas-based "scratch card" that Rekha can scratch with her finger/mouse to reveal a hidden message or image underneath
- Multiple cards with different surprises
- **Difficulty:** Medium

#### 3. **Love Language Slider**
- Five sliders for each "Love Language" (Words of Affirmation, Quality Time, etc.)
- As sliders move, a radar/spider chart visualizes the shape of your combined love profile
- **Difficulty:** Medium

#### 4. **Memory Match Card Game**
- A classic flip-and-match game where the cards have inside jokes, emoji pairs, or small photos
- Tracks number of moves, shows a celebration animation on completion
- **Difficulty:** Medium-Hard

---

### ✨ Visual / Ambient Enhancements

#### 5. **Parallax Photo Gallery / Polaroid Wall**
- Floating polaroid-style photos with handwritten-style captions that drift on scroll
- Could use actual photos of places, moments, or even hand-drawn doodles
- **Difficulty:** Medium

#### 6. **Ambient Music Toggle**
- A small floating button that plays a soft lo-fi or piano track (your "our song")
- Visualizer wave animation when playing
- **Difficulty:** Easy

#### 7. **Shooting Star / Wish System**
- Occasionally a shooting star crosses the starfield background
- Clicking it in time lets you "make a wish" — type a wish into a floating text field, which gets saved to `localStorage` and can be re-read later
- **Difficulty:** Medium

#### 8. **Aurora / Gradient Mood Ring Background**
- A slow-shifting aurora borealis gradient behind sections that changes hue based on scroll position or time of day
- Morning = warm golds, Night = deep purples
- **Difficulty:** Easy-Medium

---

### 💌 Content / Emotional Depth

#### 9. **"What I'd Tell You If..." Rotating Letters**
- A deck of cards that auto-rotates (or swipes) through hypothetical letters:
  - "If we met 10 years earlier…"
  - "If I could relive one day…"
  - "If words weren't enough…"
- Beautiful card-stack animation
- **Difficulty:** Easy-Medium

#### 10. **Shared Bucket List**
- A visual checklist of dreams/adventures you want to do together
- Items can be toggled as "done" with a celebratory confetti burst
- Categories: Travel 🌍, Food 🍕, Experiences 🎢, Milestones 💍
- **Difficulty:** Easy

#### 11. **"Our Soundtrack" Playlist Section**
- List of meaningful songs with album art, a short note about why each matters
- Each song card has a play button linking to Spotify/YouTube
- Vinyl record spin animation on hover
- **Difficulty:** Easy-Medium

#### 12. **Monthly/Anniversary Countdown**
- A beautiful circular countdown ring to the next milestone (next monthiversary, birthday, anniversary)
- Shows "X days until our __ month anniversary" with a golden ring progress indicator
- **Difficulty:** Easy

---

### 🎉 Fun / Playful

#### 13. **"Would You Rather" Couples Edition**
- Tap to flip through romantic/funny "would you rather" prompts
- "Would you rather: Have a picnic under the stars ✨ OR a movie marathon on a rainy day 🌧️"
- **Difficulty:** Easy

#### 14. **Virtual Flower Garden**
- A small interactive garden where clicking plants a new flower
- Each flower represents a day/moment — the garden grows over time
- Flowers have different colors and subtle sway animations
- **Difficulty:** Medium

#### 15. **Secret Knock / Tap Pattern Unlock**
- A hidden section that only unlocks if Rekha taps a specific rhythm/pattern (like your special knock)
- Reveals a super-secret message or photo
- **Difficulty:** Medium
